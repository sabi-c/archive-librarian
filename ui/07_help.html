Common Issues
Login Failed
Your archive.org password might be wrong, or your account isn't verified yet. Sign in to archive.org in your browser to confirm. Then go to Settings → Change credentials and re-enter.
Download Won't Start
The book might require borrowing first. Open archive.org, find the book, click "Borrow for 1 hour", copy the URL, paste it back here.
Download Fails Mid-Way
archive.org sometimes throttles downloads. Click Retry on the error screen. If it keeps failing, wait 10 minutes and try again.
Where Are My Books?
By default in ~/Books/Archive. Open Settings → Library → Books folder to see the path or change it. Click any book in the Library view to open it in macOS Preview.
App Hangs Or Freezes
Quit the app (Cmd+Q) and re-open. Your credentials and library survive — they're stored separately. If it keeps hanging, send the diagnostics below to whoever set this up for you.
