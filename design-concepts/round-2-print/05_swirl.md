# SWIRL – Dot-Matrix Archive Librarian Design

## Concept
**The dot-matrix swirl as ARIA's visual identity.** Concentric arcs of white dots arranged in a radial spiral — LIFE magazine's logo pattern becomes the unified design language throughout the interface. The swirl *is* the librarian; it breathes, rotates, and repeats at multiple scales.

## Visual DNA
- **Reference**: LIFE logo + 1970s–80s minimalist corporate identity (Vignelli, Wyman)
- **Core color**: Deep ultramarine blue (#1c4ba0) — exact match to LIFE reference
- **Accent**: Pure white (#ffffff) dots + warm cream (#f4ecd6) for secondary text
- **Texture**: Faint canvas/denim weave overlay (SVG grid at low opacity)
- **Signature motif**: Radial dot-swirls at 5+ scales — logo, activity indicators, dividers, rhythm

## Key Design Moves
1. **Hero centered** — 280px dot-swirl SVG (hand-placed polar coordinates) above wordmark, like a corporate cover
2. **Wordmark stacked** — "ARCHIVE" / "LIBRARIAN" in bold Helvetica, all caps, tight tracking, white on blue
3. **Status pulse** — Single white dot with opacity animation + "Ready to Archive" label
4. **Action button** — Solid white block on blue, bold sans-serif, minimal depth
5. **Recent list** — Numbered (01–04), hairline dividers, cream date stamps
6. **Restraint** — No chrome, no gradients except the background; the swirl *is* the decoration

## Dot-Swirl Structure
- **Outer ring** (12 dots): 9–10px diameter, evenly radial
- **Second ring** (16 dots): 7–7.5px, higher density
- **Third ring** (20 dots): 5–5.5px, tighter spiral
- **Inner ring** (24 dots): 2.5–3.5px, dense core
- **Center**: 4px + 1.5px highlight
- **Effect**: Visual gravity pulls inward; dots get smaller toward center, maintaining balance and suggesting depth

## Typography Stack
- **Wordmark**: Helvetica Neue Black, 56px, -1.5px letter-spacing, all caps
- **Tagline**: 14px, 0.8px tracking, uppercase, cream color
- **Labels**: 12–14px, 0.6–1px tracking, white, uppercase
- **Body**: System font, 14px, light weight, white

## Color Decisions
- **Deep blue**: Energy-efficient, premium, recalls 1970s print culture
- **White dots**: Maximum contrast, signify data/connection, read as "preserved"
- **Cream**: Warm, paper-like, softens the interface for longer viewing
- **No red**: Restraint — reserved for future "archived" accent only

## Responsive
- Mobile (< 600px): Swirl scaled to 220px, wordmark 42px, button padding reduced
- Desktop (900–1200px): Full visual rhythm, hero section dominates

## Mood
Monolithic. Deliberate. Like an archive's binding ledger. The swirl suggests motion without animation; the whole thing feels like it has been rotating slowly for decades.
